Why Fasal Sathi

Everything your crop needs,
held in one hand

🌱
Know your soil instantly Measures Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, pH, moisture, temperature, EC, salinity & TDS — all in one reading.
🔍
Identifies any crop from a photo Just point the camera — Fasal Sathi recognises the plant species and its common local names automatically.
🦠
Detects diseases before they spread Powered by two AI health engines (Plant.id + Crop.Health) to catch fungal, bacterial, and pest issues early.
🗣️
Speaks advice in Hindi Gives a full 130-word farming advisory out loud — fertiliser names, doses, timings — in simple village Hindi.
📱
Syncs wirelessly to your phone Connects via Bluetooth to the Fasal Sathi app and pushes every soil reading in real time.
📊
Tracks your soil health over time Stores your last 3 scans and computes a Plant Care Score so you can see if your field is improving.
🌦️
Shows live weather on the wand Displays today's forecast — temperature, rain chance, and animated weather icon — right on the OLED screen.
⚡
No app needed for basic use The wand works fully standalone — insert probe, press button, hear advice. Phone app adds extra data visibility.
🎙️
Voice-input crop name Can't find the crop in the photo? Just say its name — the wand transcribes your voice and tailors the advice.
💊
Specific treatment, not vague tips Tells you exactly: "add half a spoon of copper oxychloride in 1 litre water, spray in the morning." Not just "use pesticide."
🔋
Auto-starts on power-up No fumbling — the wand boots and is ready within 5 seconds of being switched on. No keyboard, no login.
🇮🇳
Built for Indian conditions Calibrated for Indian soil types, crops, and climate. Advisory always references local fertiliser names like Urea, DAP, MOP.
🔓
Completely free to download The mobile app is free — no subscription, no ads, no sign-up. Science belongs to farmers, not corporations.

Designed by a 15-year-old for the country Fasal Sathi is a Class X student project from Dehradun, built with the belief that every farmer deserves an AI companion.

Simple to use

From field to advice in 4 steps

🪛
Insert the probe
Push the sensor prongs into your soil. The wand stabilises for 5 seconds to get accurate readings.
🔘
Press SCAN
One button press reads all 9 soil parameters — NPK, pH, moisture, temp, EC, salinity, TDS.
📸
Photograph your crop
Point the camera at the plant. Two AI engines identify the species and check for diseases or pests.
🔊
Listen to the advice
Fasal Sathi speaks a personalised Hindi advisory — what to add, how much, and when — right out loud.
